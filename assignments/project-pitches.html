Project Pitches

Overview

The goal of this assignment is to permit those interested in pitching their ideas to attract a group of people interested in joining them. This pitch will be used to inspire and promote discussion amongst students before teams are formed.

This is your opportunity to shape your semester and to accomplish a personal research goal through class. If, after group discussion, you wish to create other pitches, you may upload them independently to the Google Drive Folder listed below.

Procedure

Prior To Class

  1. Read through this module.
  2. Create one slide summarizing an idea that can be studied through a Foldable Robotics team project
    • include points as to how your project satisfies the points discussed in the reading.
    • include any personal motivation1

During Class (~45 minutes)

  1. Break into small, 4-person groups in class

  2. Spend 2 minutes each presenting just your research question. (~8 minutes total)

  3. Spend 5 minutes discussing the merits of each question against the discussion points.

  4. Spend 10 minutes selecting one research question and improving it. As a group, create three slides:

    • One slide should introduce your research question. Include the members of your group.
    • Slide #2 should address how that particular question is interesting and worthy of study
    • Slide #3 should address how that particular question can be answered in a tractable way that fits within the definition of Foldable Robotics

    Do not spend time making your presentation pretty. Teams that form around this idea will do this later.

  5. Post your slides to this google drive folder2 so they can be reviewed offline and voted upon. Name your slides “zoom_room_name.pptx.”

  6. Report back to the main room to present your three slides to the rest of the class (3 minutes each x 7 = 21 minutes).

Submission

No canvas submission. Post your slides to this google drive folder so they can be reviewed offline and voted upon. Name your slides “zoom_room_name.pptx.”
